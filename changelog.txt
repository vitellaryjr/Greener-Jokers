1.3.0
Additions:
Added 5 new Jokers: Steam Punk, Lollipop, Musical Chairs, Take me to Your Leader, and new legendary Crawfish. These aren't the main focus by any means, as this update is mostly fixing up old Jokers whose functions I didn't particularly like, alongside some balance changes, but I was itching to do a few more
Added 3 new Decks: Neon Deck, Deck of Life, and Temple Deck. Now we're at 80 Jokers and 8 Decks!

Changes:
High Striker is reworked, it now functions like a retroactive scaling xmult joker for straight flushes. hoping it fits a similar niche to throwback now
Ferrets should no longer convert ranks to themselves when randomizing rank
Combination Lock has been reworked to not make a round take 6 extra weeks of counting before you play each hand
Norm is now one dollar cheaper
Grand Canyon now gives +mult instead of xmult, since I couldn't settle on a number for xmult that felt usable but not overpowered
Magnetic Joker is now uncommon and doesn't have the no discards stipulation. This makes it more similar to DNA but it was already the same idea anyways and DNA did it pretty well
Walk of Fame has some tweaks so that it doesn't give the seal to all the cards at the exact same time 
Cotton candy lasts an extra round and costs an extra dollar, now maybe a bit easier to use
Pinhead has been majorly reworked, as 40 chips wasn't a good enough reason to hold a 5 or 10 in hand, and just increasing the numbers didn't feel right. Now it gives mult, and discards those cards after the hand is played
Blueberries costs 2 dollars instead of 4, because I wanted to make it feel a little bit more like Dave from nubby's number factory
Reworked Landfill to be a lot more straightforward, it was always bugging me and I never really wanted to pick it up
Carnival Cannon has been nerfed
Gnome doesn't give a wheel of fortune for selling itself anymore. It is now also 4 dollars because it's dogshit, and 6 dollars was 6 too many dollars.
Crazy 8s costs 1 more dollar
Rune Stone no longer has a chance associated with it, and also costs 1 more dollar
Ealu has been fully reworked, it now gives a spectral card on final hand of round instead of scaling chips. It's also rare now
Sackboy gives 80 chips instead of 100 but is now $2 cheaper
Oil Barrel has been buffed it costs 2 less dollars now. i was thinking i never pick this card up but it's strong so i think it's just a starting price issue making it not worth being a speculative pick
Honeypot properly is perishable and gated behind gold cards
Joker Potion has been reworked and moved to rare
Reworked Shaman slightly to be less unpredictable
Minesweeper has been reworked, since realistically speaking while the concept fit the art there was 0 scenario where anyone would ever pick that up no matter how high i make the numbers, so now it's less opaque
Stained Glass has been completely changed, is now hopefully worth using since it doesn't add random cards to your deck and you have more control over it
